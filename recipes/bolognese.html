Spaghetti Bolognese

An image of spaghetti bolognese

Description

The first dish I was able to cook from scratch myself. A delicious recipe that can be frozen and stored for weeks at a time.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Tomato Bolognese Sauce
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Garlic Pepper
  • Basil
  • Spring Onion
  • Onion
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Place beef mince in pan
  2. Cut all vegetables
  3. Put vegetables in pan with beef and sauce
  4. Brown the mince and start boiling water in another pan.
  5. Add herbs to mince
  6. Boil pasta
  7. Eat
